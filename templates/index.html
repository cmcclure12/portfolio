About Me

I matriculated from Wynberg Boys’ High School in 2022 where I received colours for academics and football. Over the last three years, I have also coached the ladies’ and girls’ football teams and the U7 football team at my local club, where I was promoted to the senior first-team squad at age 16 while also playing first team football at my school in the years proceeding.

During the end of my matric year, I suffered a major setback that has impacted me both physically and mentally. Two weeks into my matric final exams I received news that I had torn my ACL and Meniscus and would therefore have to undergo reconstructive surgery.

After persevering through the remainder of my exams, struggling to pull myself together, I eventually did and have been hard at work in the gym. Building myself up with the intention of becoming better than I ever was. Using this injury as a test of my resilience, determination and discipline. If I can comeback from this setback, I can comeback from anything life throws at me

Recently I have found enjoyment in programming, which has led me to the creation of this website. I have been busy with courses teaching the fundamentals of programming and would like to continue this new found passion in a computer science degree next year. I have attached some of my projects that I have completed recently. I will continue to update them.
